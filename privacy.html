Privacy Policy – Hilwan Transport App

This privacy policy applies exclusively to the internal-use business tool 'Hilwan Transport' for managing transportation logistics, drivers, and equipment. It is not intended for public or consumer use.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Info: Owner/Driver names, contact, and login data.
  • Operational Data: Equipment type, status, profit/expenses, payments.
  • Device Info: Basic analytics like crashes or performance logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Visualize business metrics on the dashboard.
  • Track profits, expenses, equipment status, and driver payments.
  • Improve performance, UI, and fix bugs.

3. Storage & Security

  • Stored securely in cloud database (e.g., Firebase, MongoDB).
  • HTTPS encryption, role-based access control.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share data. Data is only accessed:

  • With explicit internal authorization
  • For debugging or compliance if required by law

5. Third-Party Tools

This app may use services like Firebase or MongoDB Atlas. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

  • Request data access or removal
  • Contact via internal IT or system admin

7. Children's Data

This app is for employees and business stakeholders only. It is not intended for children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be communicated internally to users/admins as needed.
